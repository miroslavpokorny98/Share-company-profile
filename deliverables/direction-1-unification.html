The Problem

The infrastructure exists. None of it speaks to each other.

7 subsea cables. 500+ km of fiber. 10 data centers. Scattered across Africa — built by different companies, running in parallel, never coordinated.

The Tension

600 million people unconnected. Not because the pipes aren't there.

Because there's no layer that makes them work as one. Abundance without coherence. Capacity without coordination. The network wants to exist — it just can't.

The Solution

Share is the coordination layer.

Not a new network built on top of what exists — the connective tissue that turns fragmented, siloed infrastructure into a single functioning ecosystem.

The Proof

Already live. Already connecting networks.

12 telco infrastructure providers. One contract. Unlimited backhaul. No upfront commits. The ecosystem is growing every day.

The Vision

Africa's shared backbone. Connected to the world.

From subsea cables to living rooms — Share enables any participant in the internet supply chain to deliver bandwidth more efficiently. This is just the beginning.
