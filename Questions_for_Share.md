### PRODUCT & BUSINESS

### COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE

1. How do you want Share to feel compared to other players in the African connectivity space? More tech-forward? More human? More institutional?

---

### BRAND & IDENTITY

1. Your current brand guidelines are in place, but you're open to a subtle refresh. What feels right about the current brand, and what feels like it no longer fits?
2. Your brand currently has a strong foundation: a typeface, a color palette, photography, and a signature graphic element. For the website, we'd extend that with motion language and data visualization style. Just wanted to confirm you're aligned on that.
3. The ribbed glass element is distinctive. How do you see it living on the new site — a recurring presence throughout, or more of a selective accent?
4. You mentioned references like terminal.industries, Stripe, Cloudflare, Apple, Starlink. What specifically draws you to each of these? Is it the visual feel, the storytelling, the way they present data, or something else?

---

### CONTENT

1. Do you have any content ready (written copy, bios, partner logos, investor logos,..), or will that need to be produced alongside the design?
2. Do you have any photos of the actual infrastructure that we could use as reference for the 3D visualizations? If not, feel free to share any keywords or visual references that capture the look and feel of your physical assets, and we'll handle the research on our end.
3. You have incredible numbers (7 subsea cables, 400+ km fiber, 8M+ people covered, 7 micro data centers). Are all of these confirmed and usable on the site, or are some still evolving?
4. Are there any ISP partner testimonials or case studies we can use? Even short quotes would go a long way on the B2B page.

---

### STORY, 3D & IMMERSIVE DIRECTION

1. What's the main message you want someone to take away from the homepage? If you think of it as a story with a beginning (the problem), rising tension (why it matters), a climax (here's the solution), and a resolution (proof it works), how would you tell that story? We'll take care of the visuals and the scroll experience, but we want to make sure the message underneath is exactly right.
2. What's the one thing you want people to remember after visiting the website?
3. When you think about the 3D experience, do you imagine something abstract and atmospheric (particles, data flowing through networks), or more literal and physical (actual data centers, cables)? Or a combination of both?
4. How important is it that the 3D elements represent real Share infrastructure vs. a more conceptual visualization of connectivity and scale?
5. How would you like us to showcase the platform on the website? Should it be more conceptual, giving visitors a sense that the product exists and what it does, or would you prefer actual UI screens and dashboards that show the interface in detail?

---

### TECHNICAL

1. Could you share access to your current Google Analytics? We're mainly interested in the desktop/mobile user ratio and the overall number of visits per month.
2. Are there any hardware or connectivity limitations we should account for in the African market (e.g. older devices, slower internet speeds)?
3. On our end, we'd build this in React/Next.js with Sanity as the CMS. Are you comfortable with that stack, or is there anything on your side that would require a different approach?
4. For hosting, we'd recommend Vercel. It pairs well with Next.js, handles global CDN delivery out of the box, and keeps performance tight. Does that work for you, or do you have any other requirements?
5. Are there any third-party integrations we should plan for? CRM, analytics, email marketing, chatbots, demo booking tools?
6. Where do the leads currently go, and what should happen after someone submits a form?
7. For the coverage map, is there an existing data source or API we'd pull from, or does that need to be built from scratch?
8. Are there any performance benchmarks beyond the sub-3-second load time? Specific Lighthouse scores, Core Web Vitals targets?
9. Will the page be English only, or are you considering other language versions?

---

### PRACTICAL / PROCESS

1. Any hard deadlines we should know about? Events, investor meetings, product launches that the site needs to be ready for?
2. Are there any legal or compliance considerations around how you present metrics, partnerships, or financial information on the site?
3. On the admin side, once we align on the direction and budget, we'd move to signing a contract and collecting a deposit so we can lock in the timeline and get started.
