## Two 3D Options

**Option A — Pre-rendered 3D Video (Image sequence on Scroll)**

- Cinematic 3D environment rendered as video, converted to image sequence playing frame-by-frame on scroll
- Examples: terminal-industries.com, getnauta.com
- Pros: Highest visual fidelity, smooth performance, faster dev cycle
- Cons: Not interactive, less wow, large file sizes, particles don't work well
- Budget: Less expensive

**Option B — Real-time 3D Environment (WebGL / Three.js)**

- Interactive 3D scene in browser (same tech as Lightweight)
- Examples: lightweight.info, 8bit.ai, blueyard.com, vkz.studio
- Pros: Truly immersive/interactive, reactive elements, stronger impression on investors/partners, loads faster on slow networks
- Cons: Heavier dev time, performance optimization needed, lower asset detail vs pre-rendered
- Budget: More expensive
