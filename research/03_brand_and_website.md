## Brand Guidelines

_From brand book — needs verification with full PDF_

- **Brand identity by:** ThinkSeb Studio
- **Tagline:** "Share, Africa's decentralized internet network."
- **Typefaces:** Aeonik Pro (main, "bold and refined") and HK Requisite (logo/supporting)
- **Color palette:** Shades of Blue
- **Gradients:** Ribbed glass & gradient effect, Bright to Dystopia Blue, Bright Blue to Black
- **Visual direction:** Dark, cinematic aesthetic. Predominantly deep blues and blacks. Brand photography features African communities, landscapes, infrastructure, and people. Strong use of atmospheric, moody photography. "Ribbed glass" texture is a signature brand element. The brand straddles tech-forward and human/community-centered.
- **Refresh stance:** Luis says they're happy with existing guidelines but open to a subtle refresh that strengthens the "physical infrastructure" dimension.
