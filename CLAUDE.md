## Key Context

**Client:** Share (share.inc) — Africa's bandwidth marketplace / hyperscale internet network. Pre-Series A. Primary audience is ISP partners (B2B), secondary is investors.

**Agency:** VKZ (vkz.studio) — the party doing design and development.

**Confirmed tech stack:** React/Next.js + Sanity CMS + Three.js/WebGL (real-time, decided Apr 2) + Vercel hosting.

**Current site:** 3 pages on Webflow. Being fully replaced with a 4-page site (Home + Suppliers + ISPs + Users).

**Key brand notes:** Typefaces are Aeonik Pro + HK Requisite. Dark cinematic aesthetic, deep blues and blacks. Apr 6 tone direction: "war room atmosphere" — muted, slightly desaturated blues, darker depth. Open to subtle refresh emphasizing physical infrastructure.

**Confirmed numbers (publicly usable as of April 1, 2026):** 7 subsea cables, 500+ km fiber, 8M+ people covered, 10 data centers, 12 telco infrastructure providers, 4,751 telecom towers accessible in Kenya (dashboard-verified).

**Budget:** $32–35K (real-time WebGL).
