## What Share Is

Share is building Africa's Hyperscale Internet Network — a bandwidth marketplace that sits between backbone infrastructure and local ISPs. They don't sell internet to end consumers. They partner with small-to-medium ISPs and give them three things: backbone bandwidth (backhaul), a management and billing platform, and financial tools to grow.

**The core differentiator:** No upfront capacity fees. Traditional ISPs commit to paying for bandwidth monthly whether or not they use it. Share eliminates that commitment entirely — instead taking a percentage of what the ISP collects from their end users. Share only earns when partners earn.

**What "hyperscale" means:** Share aggregates existing underutilized infrastructure (dark fiber, idle assets) rather than building from scratch. Coverage expands without Share laying cable.

**Parent company:** Living Twice, Inc. **CEO:** Luis Munoz Aycart. **Stage:** Pre-Series A. **HQ:** Mombasa, Kenya. **Currently active in:** Kenya (Uganda license ~2 months away).

---

## The Business Model in Plain English

1. **Physical layer:** Share places its own networking equipment in data centers, interconnecting with fiber from multiple backbone providers. ISPs plug into Share's network at a local PoP — instantly accessing all aggregated bandwidth connected to 7 subsea cables.
2. **Financial layer:** End users pay their ISP through Share's portal. Share splits the payment automatically — partner keeps ~80%, Share takes its fee. The platform is effectively an ISP bank account: balance, user payments, financial products.
3. **Long-term revenue:** Data is getting too cheap to monetize. Share's real bet is financial products — starting with lines of credit for ISPs to fund network expansion. FOREX and more to follow.

**The Stripe parallel:** Stripe didn't ask merchants to rebuild their websites — just add a module. Share is the same for ISPs: an add-on to an existing business, not a replacement.

| # | Audience | What they need from the site | Key fear / hesitation |

| 1 | **ISP partners** | Clear value prop, credibility, demo CTA | Don't know Share exists (brand awareness). Fear of disrupting a working business. |

| 3 | **End users** | Find a local ISP that uses Share (future feature) | Not yet an active audience. |

**Investor excitement:** 50,000 users by end of 2026 = fastest-growing telco ever. Sign one ISP → absorb ~2,000 users instantly. Lean, scalable, borderless model.

**ISP hesitation:** They don't know Share. Once they hear the offer (no upfront fees, plug-and-play, keep 80%), it's compelling. Getting in the room is the problem. The website is the room.
