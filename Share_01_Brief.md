# Share — The Brief

_Official, from Share's PDF_

Compiled: March 17, 2026
Project: Share website redesign (VKZ)

---

## About Share

Share is building Africa's Hyperscale Internet Network — a bandwidth marketplace that maximizes existing, underutilized internet infrastructure to connect millions of people affordably and reliably. They are a B2B2C company: partnering with small-to-medium ISPs, providing backbone bandwidth, a management and billing platform, and growth tools — with no upfront capacity fees. Share takes a small fee on revenue generated per end-user, meaning they only earn when their partners do.

Network currently includes: interconnection with 7 subsea cables, aggregated IPT from major Tier 1 providers, colocation across all major Kenya data centers, 400+ km of fiber under management, 7 micro data centers across suburban areas — covering 8M+ people.

---

## What They're Looking For

Full website redesign and development reflecting current scale and maturity. Current site (3 pages, built during pre-seed) no longer represents the business.

Three audiences:

- **Investors:** Convey infrastructure depth, maturity and scale ahead of Series A. Showcase mission, metrics, network, partners, and team.
- **B2B partners (ISPs):** Clear, compelling value proposition. Drive demo requests and sign-ups.
- **D2C users (future):** Spark interest so end-consumers seek out Share-partner ISPs. Coverage map with "Find a Provider" (coming soon).

---

## Proposed Site Structure (6–8 Pages)

**IMMERSIVE / NARRATIVE:**

1. **Homepage** — Hero with network animation or infrastructure visual, animated metrics bar, value proposition cards (For ISPs / For Users / For the Ecosystem), network map preview, partner logo bar, CTA.
2. **Share Network** — The "scale" page. Subsea cable map, fiber network visualization, data center + micro DC topology diagram, Tier 1 aggregation, hyperscale model explainer, metrics.
3. **About / Mission** — Mission statement, the connectivity gap problem, our approach, future of financial services, investor logos, press.

**FUNCTIONAL / CONVERSION:** 4. **For ISPs (B2B)** — Value proposition, traditional model pain points vs. Share model (step-by-step), feature grid, testimonials/case studies, demo request CTA. 5. **Platform** — Share Platform showcase — core features (billing, user management, payments) + roadmap (lending, FOREX, D2C marketplace). UI mockups. 7. **Contact** — Categorized inquiry form (B2B, Investor, Press, General), direct contact channels, office locations.

> Note: Page 6 is missing from the brief (jumps from 5 to 7). Possibly a "For Users" or "Coverage Map" page.

## Creative Direction

Existing brand guidelines in place, open to subtle refresh strengthening the "physical infrastructure" dimension. Site should feel like a blend between a modern tech platform (terminal.industries, Stripe, Cloudflare, Apple, Starlink) and an infrastructure company with real, tangible assets.

## Timeline & Deliverables

- **Timeline:** 2–3 months from engagement to launch
- **Scope:** Full design and development (desktop + mobile) inclusive of 3D illustration/rendering or other immersive and creative features
- **Tech:** Agency to recommend CMS (Webflow, WordPress, or headless) and interactive solution. Site must be mobile-first, fast-loading (< 3s), and SEO-optimized.
